The Worst Calculator in the World

v1.0

Five fully functional calculators, each engineered to be as inconvenient as humanly possible. Pick your suffering.

Version 1
Slider Calculator
Pick numbers from 0 to 1000. With sliders. Good luck landing on exactly 42.
Version 2
Drunk Buttons
The buttons are scared of your cursor. They run. They swap. They jiggle.
Version 3
Roman Numerals
Input only I, V, X, L, C, D, M. Output also in Roman. Like Caesar would have used.
Version 4
Math Riddle
To enter a digit, first solve a math problem that equals that digit. Recursion.
Version 5
Trust Issues
Random delays. Confirmation modals. And it lies to you 10% of the time.
